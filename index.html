Medical Terms Glossary

Understanding physiotherapy terminology in simple language

Range of Motion (ROM)

The full movement potential of a joint, usually its range of flexion and extension.

Mobilization

Manual therapy techniques to restore joint movement and reduce pain.

Proprioception

The body's ability to sense its position, movement, and action in space.

Isometric

Muscle contraction without movement, where the muscle length remains constant.

Eccentric

Muscle contraction where the muscle lengthens while under tension.

Concentric

Muscle contraction where the muscle shortens while generating force.
